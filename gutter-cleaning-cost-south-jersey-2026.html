Gutter cleaning is an essential but expensive home maintenance task. This 2026 pricing guide helps South Jersey homeowners understand costs, compare DIY versus professional options, and make informed decisions about gutter maintenance.

Factors Affecting Gutter Cleaning Cost

Primary Cost Drivers:

  • Linear feet of gutters: $1-3 per foot typically
  • Debris volume: Heavy debris = higher cost
  • Number of stories: Multi-story = additional labor
  • Roof accessibility: Complex roofs cost more
  • System complexity: Multiple downspouts, unusual designs

South Jersey-Specific Factors:

  • Pine needle buildup: Requires extra effort
  • Heavy leaf drop: Fall cleaning more expensive
  • High debris season: Summer/fall premium pricing
  • Labor rates: South Jersey 5-10% above NJ average
Cost Per Foot: Most South Jersey professionals charge $1-3 per linear foot, with seasonal variations. Heavy debris seasons (fall) may cost 20-30% more.

Seasonal Pricing Variations

Spring (April-May): $0.90-$1.50/foot (lowest season)

Summer (June-July): $1.00-$1.75/foot (moderate)

Fall (September-November): $1.50-$3.00/foot (highest season, peak demand)

Winter (December-March): $1.00-$2.00/foot (limited service availability)

DIY Gutter Cleaning Cost Analysis

DIY Material Costs:

  • Extension ladder rental: $15-$40/day
  • Gutter scoop/tools: $10-$30 (one-time)
  • Work gloves: $5-$15
  • Bags/disposal: $10-$20
  • Total material: $40-$105

DIY Labor Cost (Your Time):

  • Time required: 6-12 hours (avg home)
  • Your hourly value: $20-$50/hour reasonable
  • Time value: $120-$600
  • Total DIY cost (including labor): $160-$705

DIY vs Professional Comparison:

  • Professional cost: $200-$350 average
  • DIY material cost only: $40-$105
  • But DIY time cost: $120-$600
  • Value of safety (no ladder risk): Priceless

Getting Affordable Professional Service

Cost Reduction Strategies:

  • Bundle multiple cleanings for discount (10-15%)
  • Schedule off-season (spring): Lower rates
  • Get competitive quotes (3-5 quotes recommended)
  • Ask about package discounts
  • Spring cleaning: 20% cheaper than fall typically

When Professional Cleaning is Worth It

Professional service makes sense when:

  • You have trees close to home
  • You value your time highly
  • You want to avoid ladder safety risk
  • You have significant debris (pine needles, heavy leaves)
  • You have difficulty climbing or health issues
