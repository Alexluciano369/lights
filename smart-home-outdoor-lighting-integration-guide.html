Compatible Smart Home Platforms

Oelo permanent LED lighting systems integrate with:

  • Amazon Alexa – Voice commands and routines
  • Google Home – Voice control and automation
  • Apple HomeKit – Siri commands and scenes
  • Samsung SmartThings – Hub integration
  • IFTTT – Custom automations

Voice Control Examples

  • "Alexa, turn the house lights red and green"
  • "Hey Google, dim outdoor lights to 50%"
  • "Siri, activate party mode lighting"
  • "Alexa, turn off all outdoor lights at midnight"

Automation Ideas

Sunset Activation: Lights turn on automatically when sun sets

Security Mode: Lights activate when away, simulating occupancy

Holiday Schedules: Christmas colors from Thanksgiving to New Year's, then auto-switch to white

Motion Integration: Brighten lights when doorbell or security cameras detect motion
