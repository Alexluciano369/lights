Serving Brigantine's Barrier Island Community

Brigantine is a one-of-a-kind community — a barrier island just north of Atlantic City, bounded by the Atlantic Ocean to the east and the Brigantine Inlet and Absecon Bay to the west. This compact island city combines a relaxed beach-town atmosphere with luxury oceanfront homes, a thriving golf community, and miles of pristine shoreline. But living on a barrier island presents a unique set of challenges for homeowners, and at CleanGutters Lighting Co., we've spent over 15 years mastering the solutions.

The Brigantine environment is relentless on exterior home components. Salt spray from the Atlantic carries corrosive particles that eat through standard materials. Wind-driven sand scours unprotected surfaces. Nor'easters and tropical weather systems can dump several inches of rain in a matter of hours, testing even the most robust gutter systems. And despite the island setting, Brigantine's neighborhoods feature plenty of ornamental trees — Bradford pears, Japanese maples, and coastal pines — that contribute their own share of gutter-clogging debris.

Whether you own a year-round residence in the southern neighborhoods near the golf course, a vacation rental property along Ocean Avenue, or a family home in the quiet streets east of Brigantine Boulevard, CleanGutters Lighting Co. provides the right protection and lighting solutions for your specific property. We understand barrier island construction, the importance of corrosion-resistant materials, and the specific demands of coastal home maintenance.

Will permanent LED lights attract insects or cause issues near the beach?

Oelo permanent LED lights produce very little heat compared to traditional bulbs, which significantly reduces insect attraction.
