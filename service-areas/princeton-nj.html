GutterGlove Gutter Guards for Princeton Homes

Princeton's tree-lined streets are iconic. Towering oaks, American beeches, tulip poplars, and ornamental maples create a canopy that makes the borough one of the most picturesque towns in New Jersey. But that canopy comes with a cost: gutters that fill with leaves, seeds, catkins, and small branches multiple times a year. For Princeton's larger homes — many with complex rooflines, dormers, and multi-story facades — gutter cleaning is expensive, time-consuming, and risky.

GutterGlove gutter guards are the definitive solution. Designed with surgical-grade stainless-steel micro-mesh bonded to an anodized aluminum frame, they fit seamlessly over your existing gutters and stop debris at the surface while channeling water through:

  • Invisible protection: GutterGlove sits flush inside the gutter trough, maintaining the clean roofline Princeton homeowners expect.
  • Fine-debris filtering: Blocks not only leaves but pine pollen, maple helicopters, shingle granules, and the tiny seeds that clog standard screens.
  • Premium materials: Stainless steel and aluminum resist corrosion, UV degradation, and the freeze-thaw cycles of central New Jersey winters.
  • No-compromise warranty: A transferable lifetime warranty protects your investment — a meaningful benefit for Princeton's active real estate market.

Our installation team is accustomed to working on Princeton's high-end properties, including multi-wing estates, homes with copper gutters, and buildings with preservation-sensitive facades. We treat every property with the care it deserves.

Oelo Permanent LED Roofline Lighting in Princeton

Princeton's stately homes deserve exterior lighting that matches their architecture — not tangled strings of temporary bulbs. Oelo permanent LED roofline lighting delivers exactly that: a sleek, professionally installed system that lives under your soffit year-round and transforms on command.

Controlled through an intuitive smartphone app, Oelo lights give you complete flexibility:

  • Architectural accent lighting: Highlight your roofline with warm white or soft amber to showcase your home's design after dark.
  • Seasonal celebrations: Switch to orange for Princeton's famous Halloween festivities, red-and-green for the holidays, or orange-and-black for Princeton Tigers game day.
  • Entertainment: Set the mood for garden parties, graduation celebrations, or neighborhood gatherings with custom color palettes.
  • Security: Program lights to turn on at dusk and off at dawn, deterring unwanted visitors and lighting pathways.

The LED channels are designed to complement, not compete with, Princeton's refined architecture. During the day, they're essentially invisible. At night, they're whatever you want them to be.

Frequently Asked Questions — Princeton, NJ

Will gutter guards affect the appearance of my Princeton historic home?

Not at all. GutterGlove guards sit flush inside your existing gutters and are virtually invisible from the ground. They're an ideal solution for Princeton's historic properties where preserving architectural aesthetics is important.

How much do permanent LED roofline lights cost in Princeton?

Pricing depends on your home's roofline length and complexity. Princeton homes vary widely in size, so we provide free on-site estimates tailored to your specific property. Call 856-874-6640 to schedule yours.

Can Oelo lights be installed on slate or cedar roofs common in Princeton?

Yes. Oelo LED channels mount to the soffit or fascia, not the roofing material itself. This makes them compatible with slate, cedar shake, and other premium roofing materials found on Princeton homes, without any penetration of the roof surface.
