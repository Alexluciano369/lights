Frequently Asked Questions

Can GutterGlove handle the heavy leaf fall from Bryn Mawr's mature trees?

Yes. GutterGlove's stainless steel micro-mesh is rated for the heaviest leaf loads, including wet, matted oak leaves and pine needles — both common in Bryn Mawr. The system has been independently tested to handle over 100 inches of rain per hour, far exceeding anything Montgomery County weather delivers.

Will Oelo lighting look good on my historic Bryn Mawr stone Colonial?

Absolutely. The Oelo aluminum track is custom color-matched to your fascia, making it nearly invisible during daylight hours. We've installed on dozens of historic Main Line homes, and the result is always elegant — a clean, architectural glow that enhances your home's character rather than competing with it.

Do you serve all of Lower Merion Township and the surrounding Main Line?

Yes. We serve Bryn Mawr, Villanova, Rosemont, Haverford, Ardmore, Gladwyne, Radnor, Wayne, and the entire Main Line corridor. We also cover neighboring communities in Delaware County and across Montgomery County.

How long does a typical installation take for a Bryn Mawr home?
