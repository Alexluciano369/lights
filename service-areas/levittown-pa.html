Levittown, Pennsylvania — A Community Built on Vision

Levittown, Pennsylvania is no ordinary suburb. Conceived by William Levitt and built between 1952 and 1958, it was one of America's first and largest planned communities — a post-war experiment in affordable homeownership that shaped suburban development for generations. Today, Levittown spans parts of Bristol Township, Falls Township, Middletown Township, and Tullytown Borough, comprising over 17,000 homes across more than 40 distinct neighborhood sections with names like Appletree Hill, Goldenridge, Magnolia Hill, and Stonybrook.

CleanGutters Lighting has been serving Levittown homeowners since 2009. We understand the unique character of these homes — the original Levittowner models with their distinctive rooflines, the expanded and renovated ranches that have grown with their families over decades, and the newer infill construction that dots the neighborhood landscape. Every home in Levittown has a story, and we treat each one with the respect it deserves.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can GutterGlove be installed on an original Levittowner model home?

Yes. We've installed GutterGlove on hundreds of original Levittowner models across the community. The low-profile design works with the original roof pitch and gutter configuration. We take care to ensure the installation is secure and does not affect the home's roof structure or integrity.

How does Oelo lighting look on a ranch-style home like those in Levittown?

Ranch homes look fantastic with Oelo — the long, horizontal rooflines provide a perfect canvas. The lighting highlights the clean architectural lines that define mid-century ranches, and the color-matched track blends seamlessly with typical fascia colors. Do you serve all sections of Levittown across the different townships?
