Collingswood: Big Character, Big Trees, Big Gutter Problems

Collingswood is a gem — a walkable, restaurant-packed borough with a thriving arts scene, the beloved Collingswood Farmers Market, and block after block of beautifully maintained Craftsman bungalows, Tudor revivals, and stately Colonials. It was named one of the best downtowns in New Jersey, and residents fiercely protect the character of this tight-knit community.

But that character comes with maintenance challenges. Collingswood's tree-lined streets — the towering sycamores on Haddon Avenue, the dense oaks shading Newton Lake Park, the maples canopying the residential blocks between Collings Avenue and Browning Road — produce an extraordinary volume of leaves and debris every fall. And the borough's older homes, many built between 1900 and 1940, feature gutters that weren't designed for the volume of organic material South Jersey trees produce.

That's why Collingswood homeowners have been turning to CleanGutters Lighting Co. since 2009. We install GutterGlove micro-mesh gutter guards that permanently solve clogged gutters, and Oelo permanent LED roofline lighting that gives these beautiful homes the year-round glow they deserve.

Protecting Collingswood Basements and Foundations

One of the most common complaints we hear from Collingswood homeowners is basement moisture. Many homes in the borough sit on relatively flat lots with limited natural drainage, and the soil is heavy clay that doesn't absorb water well. When gutters overflow, water pools against the foundation and finds its way inside. Properly functioning gutters with GutterGlove guards ensure rainwater flows through your downspouts and into your drainage system — not your basement.
