GutterGlove Gutter Guards: Engineered for Hammonton's Pine Country

Hammonton sits at the edge of the Pine Barrens, which means pine needles are a year-round gutter menace. Unlike deciduous leaves that fall mainly in autumn, pine needles shed continuously — clogging gutters in spring, summer, and fall. Standard gutter screens and foam inserts can't handle pine needles because the needles are thin enough to slip right through. GutterGlove's micro-mesh technology solves this problem definitively.
