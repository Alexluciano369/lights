GutterGlove Gutter Guards for Trenton Properties

Trenton's urban tree canopy is one of the city's best features — towering oaks, maples, and sycamores line streets from Hiltonia to Chambersburg, from the Ewing border to South Trenton along the Delaware. But those beautiful trees dump enormous volumes of leaves, seeds, and twigs into residential gutters every fall, and Trenton's older homes are especially vulnerable to the consequences.

When gutters clog, water cascades down siding, pools at foundations, and seeps into basements — a serious concern for Trenton's many century-old row homes and colonials. GutterGlove gutter guards eliminate the problem at the source:

  • Micro-mesh filtration: Surgical-grade stainless steel keeps out leaves, pine needles, maple helicopters, and roofing granules.
  • Historic-home friendly: Installs over existing gutters without structural modification — perfect for Trenton's older architecture.
  • Heavy-rain capacity: Engineered to handle intense downpours that overwhelm standard open gutters, protecting your foundation during Mercer County's summer storms.
  • Lifetime protection: Backed by a manufacturer warranty that covers materials and performance for the life of your home.

Whether you own a brick row home in the Mill Hill Historic District or a split-level in the Hamilton border neighborhoods, our crews deliver a clean, professional installation — typically in one day.

Oelo Permanent LED Roofline Lighting in Trenton

Trenton's neighborhoods come alive during the holidays — from the decorated porches on Hamilton Avenue to the community light displays at Cadwalader Park. But temporary string lights are a hassle, an expense, and a safety hazard. Oelo permanent LED roofline lighting gives Trenton homeowners a better way to light up their homes, any time of year.

Installed once beneath your roofline soffit, Oelo's sleek LED channels are invisible during the day. At night, they transform your home's exterior with customizable color and brightness, all controlled from a smartphone app:

  • Holiday themes: Toggle between classic Christmas, Halloween, St. Patrick's Day, and more — or create your own palettes.
  • Everyday elegance: Warm white for dinner parties, soft amber for fall evenings, or crisp white year-round for a polished look.
  • Community spirit: Match your neighbors for block party lighting or coordinate with local events in the Trenton area.
  • Smart scheduling: Automate on/off times so lights greet you when you arrive home and shut off to save energy overnight.

The system is rated IP67 for weather resistance, handling Trenton's freeze-thaw cycles, heavy rain, and summer humidity without performance issues.
