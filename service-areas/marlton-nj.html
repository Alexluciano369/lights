The Real Cost of Neglecting Your Gutters in Marlton

Clogged gutters aren't just annoying — they're expensive. Water that overflows your gutters erodes foundation soil, seeps into basements (a common problem in Marlton's clay-heavy soils), damages siding, and creates breeding ground for mosquitoes. In winter, ice dams form along clogged gutter lines and can rip off fascia boards or push water under your shingles. A one-time gutter guard installation costs a fraction of the water damage repairs we've seen Marlton homeowners face.
