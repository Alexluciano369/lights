Philadelphia Homes Need Gutter Protection That Works

Philadelphia is a city of neighborhoods — and a city of trees. From the towering oaks along Wissahickon Creek in Roxborough to the historic sycamores of Chestnut Hill, the mature sugar maples in Mount Airy, and the London planes lining streets in Center City and South Philadelphia, Philly's urban canopy is one of its greatest assets. But for homeowners, that canopy creates a year-round gutter maintenance challenge.

Philadelphia receives an average of 44 inches of rain annually, plus regular snow and ice events from November through March. Add in the aggressive leaf drop from the city's dense tree cover — not to mention the pollen, seed pods, and debris that blow through from Fairmount Park and the river corridors — and your gutters are fighting a losing battle every season.

Oelo Permanent LED Lighting: Philadelphia, Illuminated

Philadelphia loves its light shows — from the Wanamaker light show to the Electrical Spectacle at Franklin Square to the Boathouse Row illumination. Now you can bring that same energy to your own home with Oelo permanent LED roofline lighting.

Oelo lights are professionally installed once and remain on your roofline permanently. The low-profile aluminum channel integrates seamlessly with your home's trim, virtually disappearing during the day. At night, you control the show:

  • Eagles green for game day — show your Philly pride every Sunday (and Monday, and Thursday) without digging out the holiday lights
  • 16 million color options — red, white, and blue for the Fourth; orange for Halloween; warm white for an elegant everyday glow
  • App-controlled scheduling — lights on at dusk, off at midnight, or on all night for security; adjust from your phone without leaving the couch
  • IP67 weather rating — built for Philadelphia's full range of conditions, from August heat and humidity to January ice storms
  • Curb appeal and safety — a well-lit home exterior deters opportunistic crime and makes your block look fantastic
