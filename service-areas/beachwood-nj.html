Trusted Home Services in Beachwood, New Jersey

Beachwood is one of Ocean County's most well-established small boroughs — a tight-knit community of approximately 11,000 residents situated along the southern bank of the Toms River, just a few miles inland from the Jersey Shore. Incorporated in 1917 as a planned residential community, Beachwood has maintained its character as a quiet, family-oriented town with tree-lined streets, a strong sense of community pride, and homes that range from original 1920s bungalows to mid-century ranches and modern colonials.

Beachwood's location along the Toms River gives many homes beautiful waterfront views — but it also creates specific maintenance challenges. River-adjacent properties deal with higher humidity levels than inland homes. The mature trees that shade Beachwood's streets — including the towering oaks and maples in the borough's older neighborhoods — produce significant seasonal debris. And Beachwood's proximity to both the ocean and the Pine Barrens means the town gets the combined effects of coastal storm patterns and the heavy pollen and needle drop characteristic of the region.
