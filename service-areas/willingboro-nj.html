When Levitt & Sons built Willingboro, they planted trees throughout every neighborhood to create the suburban paradise buyers dreamed of. Gutters that fill up fast and stay clogged.

Willingboro's original Levitt homes — the Ranchers, Cape Cods, and Colonials — were built with standard aluminum gutters that were never designed to handle this much debris. Many homeowners here have tried cheap gutter screens or foam inserts, only to find them failing within a season or two. The problem isn't the gutter — it's the volume and variety of debris coming off Willingboro's mature canopy.
