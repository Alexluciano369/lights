Gutter Protection for Florence’s Mix of Historic and Modern Homes

Florence Township occupies a distinctive stretch along the Delaware River in Burlington County — a community where 19th-century riverfront homes sit within a few miles of modern subdivisions and everything in between. This architectural diversity is part of Florence’s character, but it also means that gutter protection needs vary dramatically from house to house.

The older sections of Florence, particularly near the riverfront and in the original township grid, feature homes with steep-pitched roofs, ornate fascia details, and sometimes original half-round gutters. These historic homes benefit enormously from properly installed gutter guards because their original gutter systems — while often still solid — weren’t designed to handle decades of unmanaged leaf and debris accumulation. A single season of clogged gutters on a historic home can lead to fascia rot, soffit damage, and basement water intrusion that becomes exponentially more expensive to fix than to prevent.

Meanwhile, Florence’s newer developments — the subdivisions built from the 1980s onward — face a different challenge: volume. Larger homes with longer rooflines generate more rainwater runoff, and the mature landscaping that’s grown in around these neighborhoods means heavy seasonal debris loads. GutterGlove gutter guards handle both scenarios. The stainless steel micro-mesh is equally effective on a Victorian-era roof pitch as it is on a modern colonial with 200 linear feet of gutter.

Why Florence’s River-adjacent Location Matters

Living near the Delaware River means Florence gets the full spectrum of Mid-Atlantic weather — humid summers that accelerate organic debris decomposition in gutters, windy autumns that strip trees bare in a matter of days, and winter freeze-thaw cycles that turn a minor gutter blockage into an ice dam hazard. GutterGlove’s all-metal construction and secure fastening system are engineered precisely for these conditions.

Oelo Permanent LED Lighting — A Modern Upgrade for Florence Homes

Florence is a township where homes span two centuries of architecture, and Oelo permanent LED lighting enhances every style. On a historic riverfront Victorian, warm white roofline lighting highlights the architectural details — the gable trim, the dormer lines, the wraparound porch roof. On a newer colonial in a subdivision, full RGB color capability turns your home into the neighborhood showpiece for every holiday and event.

The system installs cleanly. We mount a powder-coated aluminum channel along your fascia board, tucked just under the drip edge. The LED strip sits inside, nearly invisible from the street during the day. At night, the lights wash downward along your exterior walls, creating an ambiance that’s simultaneously elegant and welcoming.

For Florence families, the lifestyle benefits are immediate. No more hauling extension ladders out of the garage every November. No more testing dead bulb strings and making trips to the hardware store. No more standing on frozen ground in December trying to clip lights to gutters. With Oelo, you set your holiday colors from your couch. Warm white for everyday evenings. Orange glow for Halloween week. Red and green for Christmas. Green for Eagles Sunday. Red, white, and blue for Memorial Day and the Fourth. The app handles everything — schedules, color changes, brightness levels, and custom patterns.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you install gutter guards on a historic home without damaging the original architecture?

Yes. GutterGlove guards install on top of your existing gutters with hidden fasteners — no modifications to your roof, fascia, or architectural trim. For Florence’s historic homes, we take extra care during installation to preserve original materials and match any visible components to the home’s aesthetic. Our crew has extensive experience with period-appropriate installations.

Does Oelo permanent lighting work with multi-story homes?

Absolutely. We install Oelo on homes of all sizes, including the two- and three-story properties found in Florence. Our crew uses proper ladder safety protocols and commercial-grade equipment to reach even the highest rooflines. The LED strips are continuous-run capable, so long fascia lengths are handled seamlessly without visible breaks or connectors.

How do I control the Oelo lights? Do I need special wiring?

Oelo runs on standard low-voltage wiring (similar to landscape lighting). We install a controller box near an exterior outlet, and the system connects to your home Wi-Fi. You control everything through the Oelo smartphone app — available for both iPhone and Android. No special wiring, no electrician visit, no disruption to your home’s electrical panel.

What’s the warranty on GutterGlove and Oelo products?

GutterGlove offers a transferable lifetime warranty on their stainless steel micro-mesh guards. Oelo provides a multi-year warranty on the LED strips, controllers, and power supplies. CleanGutters Lighting Co. also backs every installation with our own workmanship guarantee. If something isn’t right, we come back and make it right — no runaround, no excuses.
