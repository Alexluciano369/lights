Carneys Point occupies a unique position in South Jersey — it’s the gateway to Delaware, sitting just east of the Delaware Memorial Bridge where I-295 and the New Jersey Turnpike converge. Routine gutter cleaning in Carneys Point isn’t just an inconvenience — it’s a safety concern. Our stainless steel mesh catches it all while maintaining peak water flow, even during the summer thunderstorms that roll up the Delaware River corridor.

Will GutterGlove guards work with the high winds near the Delaware Memorial Bridge?

Yes. GutterGlove guards are mechanically fastened to your existing gutters — not just pressed in or friction-fit. How do Oelo lights compare to traditional holiday string lights?

Oelo permanent lighting replaces string lights entirely. Instead of untangling and hanging lights every November and taking them down in January, you install Oelo once and control it from your phone forever. Over 5-7 years, Oelo typically pays for itself compared to buying new string lights, paying for seasonal installation and removal, and replacing failed bulbs.
