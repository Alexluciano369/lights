The Hidden Costs of Clogged Gutters in Moorestown

Many Moorestown homeowners don't realize the damage clogged gutters cause until it's too late. Water that can't flow through gutters overflows against your foundation, seeps into basements (a persistent issue with the clay-heavy soil common in Burlington County), stains siding, and erodes landscaping. In winter, standing water in clogged gutters freezes into ice dams that can tear off fascia boards and drive water under your roof shingles. Professional gutter cleaning in Moorestown runs $200-400 per visit, multiple times a year. GutterGlove pays for itself within a few years — and then protects your home for life.
