Its position directly bordering Northeast Philadelphia makes it one of the busiest residential corridors in Lower Bucks County.

🏠 GutterGlove Gutter Guards — Built for Bensalem's Climate

Frequently Asked Questions

Does GutterGlove work on the flat-roof sections common in some Bensalem homes?

GutterGlove gutter guards are designed for sloped roofs with standard gutters, which covers the vast majority of Bensalem homes — Colonials, ranches, splits, and Capes. If your home has a flat roof section with internal drains, we can assess your full gutter and drainage system during your free estimate and recommend the best solution.

Can Oelo lighting handle humidity and river-adjacent conditions near the Delaware?

Yes. Oelo's LED modules are fully sealed and weatherproof. The aluminum track is powder-coated and corrosion-resistant. We have installations on waterfront properties along the Delaware River in Andalusia that have performed flawlessly through years of humid summers and coastal-style weather exposure.

Do you cover all of Bensalem Township, including Trevose and Andalusia?

Absolutely. We serve every neighborhood and ZIP code within Bensalem Township — Trevose (19053), Andalusia, Cornwells Heights, Eddington, and the Neshaminy area. We also cover all surrounding Lower Bucks County communities including Bristol, Levittown, and Feasterville-Trevose.

What's the best time of year to install gutter guards in Bensalem?

We install year-round, but late summer and early fall (August through October) are ideal — you'll have protection in place before the heavy leaf drop begins. Spring is also popular so gutters are protected ahead of summer thunderstorm season. We'll work with your schedule regardless of the season.
