Frequently Asked Questions

Do gutter guards work with the heavy leaf fall we get in rural Alloway?

Absolutely. GutterGlove's stainless steel micro-mesh is specifically engineered for heavy debris loads. The micro-mesh sits at an angle so leaves, acorns, and pine needles slide off while water drains through. We've installed these systems on wooded properties throughout Salem County with excellent results — including homes with mature oak canopies dropping hundreds of pounds of leaves each fall.

Can Oelo permanent lighting handle the humidity and weather in southwestern NJ?

Yes. Oelo LED systems are built for permanent outdoor installation and are fully weatherproof — rated to handle everything from coastal humidity to freezing winter temperatures. The LEDs are commercial-grade and the track system is engineered to withstand years of New Jersey seasons, including the damp conditions common around Alloway's creeks and wetlands.

Do you serve the entire Alloway Township area?

We serve all of Alloway Township and the surrounding areas, including the village proper, properties along Alloway-Woodstown Road, the lake area, and the rural roads throughout the township. If you're within Salem County, give us a call — we likely cover your area.

How long does installation take for a typical Alloway home?

Most gutter guard installations on a typical Alloway home take one day. Permanent lighting installations typically take one to two days depending on the size of the home and the complexity of the roofline. We'll give you a precise timeline during your free estimate.
