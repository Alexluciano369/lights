Cape May is the crown jewel of the Jersey Shore—a National Historic Landmark city renowned for its meticulously preserved Victorian architecture, pristine beaches, and year-round charm. From the grand painted ladies along Beach Avenue to the cozy bed-and-breakfasts tucked into side streets near the Washington Street Mall, Cape May's homes are as beautiful as they are vulnerable. Constant salt air from the Atlantic and Delaware Bay, wind-driven sand, seasonal nor'easters, and the humidity that comes with being at the southernmost tip of New Jersey all take a serious toll on gutters, rooflines, and exterior fixtures.

CleanGutters Lighting Co. has been serving South Jersey homeowners since 2009, and we understand the unique demands of coastal properties. We bring two premium solutions to Cape May: GutterGlove micro-mesh gutter guards built with corrosion-resistant materials that withstand salt air, and Oelo permanent LED roofline lighting that adds year-round elegance to your home without compromising its historic character.

GutterGlove Gutter Guards: Built to Resist Cape May's Salt Air

Coastal living is beautiful, but it's brutal on metal. Standard aluminum and galvanized steel gutter guards corrode quickly in Cape May's salt-laden atmosphere—pitting, rusting, and failing within a few years. That leaves homeowners either replacing cheap guards repeatedly or climbing ladders to clean clogged gutters by hand, fighting wind-blown sand, bird nests, and debris from the elm and sycamore trees that shade many Cape May streets.

Our GutterGlove system is engineered differently. The micro-mesh is surgical-grade stainless steel—the same alloy used in marine environments—bonded to an anodized aluminum frame with a protective oxide layer. This combination resists salt air corrosion for decades, making it the ideal gutter guard for any Cape May home, whether it's a year-round residence on Lafayette Street or a seasonal rental near Convention Hall.

Why Cape May Homes Need GutterGlove

  • Salt air corrosion resistance — Surgical-grade stainless steel and anodized aluminum stand up to decades of coastal exposure without rusting, pitting, or degrading.
  • Sand and debris filtration — Wind-driven sand, bird nesting material, and tree debris are constant in Cape May. GutterGlove's fine mesh keeps it all out.
  • Vacation home protection — Many Cape May properties sit unoccupied for months during the off-season. Gutter guards ensure drainage works perfectly even when no one's home to notice a clog.
  • Historic home preservation — Overflowing gutters cause water damage to ornate Victorian woodwork, decorative trim, and painted exteriors. GutterGlove protects these irreplaceable details.

Oelo Permanent LED Lighting for Cape May's Victorian Architecture

Cape May's Victorians are famous for their intricate gingerbread trim, wraparound porches, turrets, and multi-gabled rooflines. These architectural details look spectacular when properly lit—and permanent LED roofline lighting is the modern way to do it without the hazards and headaches of temporary string lights.

Oelo permanent LED lights install once along your roofline in a slim, low-profile track that follows every contour of your home's architecture. During the day, the track is virtually invisible. At night, it transforms your home with millions of color options—controlled from your smartphone. Set a warm white glow to accentuate your Victorian's trim details, switch to festive colors for Cape May's famous Victorian Christmas celebrations, or program a soft coastal blue-and-white theme for summer evenings.

Why Permanent Lighting Is Perfect for Cape May

  • Enhances Victorian architecture — Highlight turrets, gables, and decorative trim with precisely placed LED light that accentuates your home's best features.
  • Weatherproof for coastal conditions — Oelo's IP68-rated fixtures are sealed against salt spray, driving rain, and sand. No corrosion, no water intrusion.
  • Vacation rental appeal — A beautifully lit home photographs better, earns stronger reviews, and commands higher nightly rates on platforms like VRBO and Airbnb.
  • Victorian Christmas ready — Cape May's annual Victorian Christmas festival draws thousands. Permanent lighting means your home is part of the spectacle with zero setup effort.
  • Off-season security — A well-lit roofline deters break-ins during the quiet winter months when seasonal homes are vacant.
