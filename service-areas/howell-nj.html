GutterGlove Gutter Guards Built for Howell's Wooded Lots

If you live in Howell, you know the drill: every October the canopy turns gorgeous, and then it dumps thousands of leaves into your gutters. Pine needles follow all winter. Spring brings pollen, seed pods, and helicopter seeds from the red maples. By the time summer arrives, your gutters are packed solid—and that standing water is an invitation for mosquitoes, fascia rot, and foundation erosion.

Our GutterGlove system solves the problem permanently. Surgical-grade stainless steel micro-mesh sits atop an anodized aluminum frame that mounts directly over your existing gutters. Nothing gets in except water—not leaves, not pine needles, not shingle grit. The result: gutters that flow freely every day of every season, no cleaning required.

Why GutterGlove Is the Right Choice for Howell

  • Heavy pine needle protection — Howell's abundant pine forests shed needles year-round. GutterGlove's fine mesh blocks even the smallest needles that pass through cheap gutter screens.
  • Handles large lot runoff — Bigger roofs on Howell's spacious properties collect more water. GutterGlove's high-flow design handles intense summer downpours without overflow.
  • Prevents wildlife nesting — Squirrels, birds, and wasps love unprotected gutters. Micro-mesh eliminates nesting opportunities entirely.
  • Protects your investment — With home values in Howell climbing steadily, protecting your roof, fascia, and foundation from water damage preserves equity.

Oelo Permanent LED Roofline Lighting for Howell Homes

Howell homeowners take pride in their properties. From the well-maintained neighborhoods near Howell Park to the newer developments along Preventorium Road, curb appeal matters here. Oelo permanent LED roofline lighting takes that curb appeal to another level—without the hassle of seasonal string lights.

Installed once along your roofline, eaves, or garage line, Oelo's low-profile aluminum track houses individually addressable LEDs that produce millions of colors. Control everything from your phone: warm white for a subtle evening glow, festive patterns for the holidays, team colors on game day, or a dynamic light show for a backyard celebration. The fixtures are rated IP68 for full weather resistance, and they draw minimal power thanks to efficient LED technology.

Permanent Lighting Benefits for Howell Homeowners

  • Eliminate ladder risks — No more climbing two stories every November and January to hang and remove holiday lights.
  • 365-day curb appeal — A soft warm glow every evening makes your home stand out on the street. Change colors for any occasion instantly via the app.
  • Security and visibility — Well-lit rooflines deter trespassers and make your property safer at night, especially on Howell's larger, more secluded lots.
  • Energy efficient — Oelo LEDs consume a fraction of the power of traditional incandescent string lights, keeping your PSE&G bill in check.
