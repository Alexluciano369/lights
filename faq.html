Gutter Guard Questions

Do gutter guards really work?

Yes — when professionally installed. Micro-mesh gutter guards (especially stainless steel like GutterGlove) block leaves, pine needles, seeds, and pests while letting water flow freely. We've installed 1,000+ homes across Cherry Hill, Voorhees, and South Jersey with a lifetime no-clog guarantee.

How much do gutter guards cost in South Jersey?

Most homes in Cherry Hill and Camden County range from $1,500–$4,500 depending on home size, linear footage, and gutter condition. Every installation includes a free in-home assessment with transparent pricing — no hidden fees. See our detailed pricing guide →

What type of gutter guard is best?

Stainless steel micro-mesh (specifically GutterGlove systems) is the gold standard. It handles over 29 inches of rain per hour, blocks the smallest debris including pine needles and shingle grit, and comes with a lifetime transferable warranty. Avoid plastic, foam, and brush-style guards — they fail within 2–3 years in New Jersey weather.

How long does gutter guard installation take?

Most homes are completed in a single day. Our certified team handles everything from gutter inspection to final cleanup. We install year-round in South Jersey — weather permitting. Read about the best time to install →

Do I still need to clean my gutters with guards installed?

No — that's the entire point. With micro-mesh gutter guards professionally installed, you never climb a ladder to clean gutters again. The no-clog guarantee is backed by a lifetime warranty. Compare cleaning costs vs. guard investment →

Will gutter guards cause ice dams in winter?

No — properly installed micro-mesh guards actually help reduce ice dam formation by keeping debris out and allowing water to flow freely through your gutter system. Learn how gutter guards prevent ice dams →

How does GutterGlove compare to LeafFilter?

Both use micro-mesh technology, but GutterGlove offers the same or better protection typically at a lower price point. Our installations are done by the owner, not subcontractors. Read our detailed comparison →

What's the warranty on your gutter guards?

Lifetime transferable no-clog warranty. If the system ever clogs, we clean it free. If anything fails, we replace it. The warranty transfers to the next homeowner — which helps your home's resale value.

Permanent Outdoor Lighting Questions

What are permanent outdoor lights?

Permanent under-soffit LED lighting that installs once and stays year-round. You control colors and patterns from your smartphone via the Oelo Evolution app. Use them for Christmas, Halloween, July 4th, security lighting, or everyday curb appeal.

How much does permanent outdoor lighting cost?

Most South Jersey homes range from $3,000–$8,000 depending on linear footage, channel type (acrylic or metal), and installation complexity. Includes a free design consultation. See our full pricing breakdown →

How long do Oelo permanent lights last?

Rated for 100,000 hours — that's approximately 34 years of 8-hour nightly use. Each LED is UL certified, IP68 water submersible rated, and backed by Oelo's warranty. You'll likely replace your roof before these lights.

Can I change the colors?

Yes — the Oelo Evolution app gives you control over 16+ million colors and hundreds of pre-set patterns. Schedule automatic changes, adjust brightness, and control multiple properties from anywhere in the world.

Are permanent lights energy efficient?

Yes. Premium LEDs use a fraction of the electricity of traditional holiday lights or floodlights. Most homeowners report negligible impact on their electric bill even with nightly use. Read our LED energy savings guide →

Will the lights look visible during the day?

No — the system is designed to be practically invisible during daylight. The fixtures mount flush under the soffit or fascia in color-matched channels (acrylic or metal). By day, you see nothing. By night, your home becomes a showpiece.

Can permanent lighting improve home security?

Absolutely. Programmed security lighting deters intruders, illuminates dark areas around your home, and makes your property look occupied even when you're away. Learn about lighting and home security →

Do permanent lights increase home value?

Yes — buyers love upgraded exterior lighting. Improved curb appeal, security benefits, and a permanent system that transfers with the home are strong selling points. See how lighting impacts home value →

What's the difference between Oelo, Trimlight, and Gemstone?

Oelo offers patented mounting systems (acrylic and metal channels), UL certification, IP68 waterproof rating, and the Evolution smart app. We install Oelo exclusively because of its build quality and reliability. Read our full brand comparison →
