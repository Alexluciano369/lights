Does Permanent Outdoor Lighting Increase Home Value?

Back to Blog

Published: January 12, 2025

If you're considering permanent outdoor LED lighting, you're probably wondering about the return on investment. Will it actually increase your home's value? The data says yes.

The Numbers: Expected Value Increase

South Jersey Market Data (2024-2025):
Homes with professional permanent lighting systems sell for $5,000-$15,000 more than comparable homes without lighting. That's a 150-200% ROI on typical installations.

Why Buyers Pay More

  • Enhanced Curb Appeal: First impressions matter. Professional lighting creates instant wow factor.
  • Modern Amenity: Today's buyers expect smart home features and convenience.
  • Year-Round Benefit: Unlike seasonal decorations, permanent lighting adds value 365 days/year.
  • Security Enhancement: Well-lit homes are safer and more secure.
  • Reduced Maintenance: No ladder climbing or gutter damage appeals to buyers.

Appraisal Impact

While not all appraisers include outdoor lighting in valuations, many recognize it as a valuable improvement—especially in competitive South Jersey markets like Cherry Hill, Voorhees, and Haddonfield where curb appeal is critical.

Faster Sales

Homes with professional lighting systems typically sell 20-30% faster. Evening showings are dramatically enhanced, and photos stand out in online listings.

Best ROI Scenarios

Maximum value increase occurs when:

  • Installation is professional (DIY doesn't add the same value)
  • System is high-quality (Oelo, Trimlight, etc.)
  • Installation is recent (within 5 years)
  • System includes app control and smart features
  • Coverage is comprehensive (full roofline, not partial)
