Gutter Cleaning Costs vs. Gutter Guards: The Real Math

Back to Blog

Published: January 12, 2025

Every South Jersey homeowner faces the same question: Should I keep paying for annual gutter cleaning or invest in gutter guards? Let's analyze the real costs over time.

Annual Gutter Cleaning Costs in South Jersey

  • Basic cleaning (2x/year): $300-$600
  • Complex homes with trees: $600-$1,200
  • Emergency cleanings: $150-$300 each
  • Repairs from clogs: $200-$2,000
10-Year Total: $3,000-$12,000+ in cleaning costs alone, plus potential damage from missed cleanings.

Gutter Guard Investment

  • GutterGlove professional installation: $1,500-$4,000
  • Lifetime no-clog warranty
  • No more cleaning costs
  • Protection against water damage

Break-Even Analysis

With annual cleaning costs of $400-$600, gutter guards typically pay for themselves in 3-7 years. After that, you save $400-$600 annually for the life of your home.

25-year savings: $10,000-$15,000+

Hidden Costs of Regular Cleaning

  • Time scheduling and home access
  • Risk of damage from ladder placement
  • Water damage between cleanings
  • Foundation issues from overflow
  • Landscape damage
