When Should You Install Gutter Guards in South Jersey?

Back to Blog

Published: January 12, 2025

Timing matters when installing gutter guards. Here's your complete seasonal guide for South Jersey.

Fall: The Ideal Season (September-November)

Why Fall is Best:

  • Protects gutters before winter ice and snow
  • Prevents leaf accumulation from fall foliage
  • Comfortable weather for installation crews
  • Ready for holiday lighting if desired
  • Allows time to test before winter storms

Spring: Second Best Option (March-May)

Spring installation prepares your home for summer storms and heavy rains. It's also when homeowners notice winter damage that could have been prevented.

Winter & Summer: Possible But Not Ideal

While gutter guards can be installed year-round, extreme temperatures make installation more challenging and may extend project timelines.
