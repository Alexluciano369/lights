OELO vs Gemstone vs TrimLight: LED System Comparison

Published on November 1, 2024 | By CleanGutters Lighting

The Three Leaders in Permanent LED Lighting

OELO, Gemstone, and TrimLight are the top-tier permanent outdoor LED lighting systems. Each uses proprietary technology and design philosophy, offering different strengths and benefits for homeowners seeking high-end outdoor lighting.

Understanding these differences helps you select the system best suited to your vision and budget.

System Overview

Feature OELO Gemstone TrimLight
Technology Smart addressable LEDs Modular color-changing Flexible ribbon lights

OELO System Details

Technology: Smart addressable LED nodes that can control individual colors in synchronized sequences

Installation: Nodes mounted on fascia boards and connected via low-voltage wire

Key Features:

  • Individual control of each light node
  • Synchronized animations across entire system
  • Weather-resistant exterior design
  • App-based smartphone control
  • Integration with smart home systems

Color and Effects:

  • 16 million+ color combinations
  • Dynamic effects and animations
  • Preset scenes for holidays and occasions
  • Custom color programming

Cost Range (South Jersey): $3,500-$5,500 installed (typical home)

Gemstone System Details

Technology: Modular accent lights with color-changing capability

Installation: Individual lights strategically placed for architectural highlighting

Key Features:

  • Focuses on accent lighting (architectural features, landscaping)
  • Modular design allows customized layouts
  • Color-changing RGB technology
  • Wireless app control
  • Voice command compatible

Color and Effects:

  • Full RGB spectrum control
  • Dynamic color transitions
  • Zone-based control (different zones different colors)
  • Holiday-specific presets

Cost Range (South Jersey): $2,800-$4,500 installed (typical home)

TrimLight System Details

Technology: Flexible LED ribbon lights installed along roof lines and architectural edges

Installation: Continuous ribbon of lights following home perimeter

Key Features:

  • Flexible ribbon follows any roofline contour
  • Continuous lighting vs. individual nodes
  • Wide color range and effects
  • Smartphone app control
  • Geofencing and automation

Color and Effects:

  • Millions of color options
  • Flowing animation effects
  • Synchronized patterns
  • Mood lighting modes

Cost Range (South Jersey): $3,000-$5,000 installed (typical home)

Design Philosophy Comparison

OELO: Individual accent nodes that provide dramatic highlight effects on specific features (typically 10-20 nodes per home)

Gemstone: Strategic placement of multi-function lights that serve both ambient and accent purposes (typically 8-16 lights per home)

TrimLight: Continuous ribbon perimeter lighting that outlines the entire home silhouette (typically 100-300 feet of ribbon)

Key Distinction: OELO emphasizes dramatic accents, Gemstone emphasizes flexible zones, TrimLight emphasizes complete perimeter coverage.

Installation Complexity

OELO Installation:

  • Requires fascia access and mounting points
  • Typically 8-10 hours installation
  • Wiring integration can be complex
  • Professional installation recommended

Gemstone Installation:

  • More flexible placement options
  • Typically 6-8 hours installation
  • Wireless system simplifies wiring
  • DIY possible but professional recommended

TrimLight Installation:

  • Requires secure ribbon attachment
  • Typically 10-14 hours installation (perimeter distance)
  • Professional installation essential
  • Most labor-intensive of three options

Maintenance and Durability

OELO: Excellent durability, minimal maintenance, 10-15 year lifespan, replaceable components

Gemstone: Excellent durability, minimal maintenance, 10-15 year lifespan, modular replacement possible

TrimLight: Good durability, potential wear at connection points, 8-12 year lifespan, ribbon sections replaceable

Smart Home Integration

OELO:

  • Compatible with most smart home platforms
  • Voice control available
  • Complex automation possible

Gemstone:

  • Strong smart home integration
  • Extensive voice control support
  • Zones can activate independently

TrimLight:

  • Good smart home compatibility
  • Voice command supported
  • Automation features robust

Aesthetic Results

OELO: Dramatic accent lighting on specific architectural features; creates visual interest through selective highlighting

Gemstone: Strategic uplighting and accent combinations; focuses on sculpting features with light

TrimLight: Complete home outline with continuous light; creates strong silhouette and perimeter definition

Cost-Benefit Analysis

OELO Best For: Homes with distinctive architectural features; homeowners wanting precise control; dramatic accent effect desired

Gemstone Best For: Homes with varied landscaping; homeowners wanting flexibility; balanced accent and ambient lighting

TrimLight Best For: Homes wanting complete perimeter coverage; contemporary homes with clean rooflines; homeowners wanting simplicity

Making Your Selection

Consider these factors:

  • Your home's architectural style
  • Desired lighting effect (accent vs. perimeter)
  • Budget constraints
  • Smart home integration needs
  • Installation complexity preference

Choose Your Permanent Lighting Solution

All three systems represent the pinnacle of permanent outdoor LED technology. The best choice depends on your home's unique characteristics and lighting vision.
