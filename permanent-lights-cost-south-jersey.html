2026 South Jersey Permanent Lighting Pricing

Permanent outdoor LED lighting systems have become more accessible as prices have stabilized in 2026. This comprehensive pricing guide helps South Jersey homeowners understand costs and make informed investment decisions.

System Type and Pricing Breakdown

System Type Typical Cost Range Installation Time Lifespan
Entry-Level White LED $1,500-$2,500 4-6 hours 10-15 years
Mid-Range Color System $3,000-$4,500 6-8 hours 12-15 years
Premium System (Gemstone) $3,500-$5,000 8-10 hours 12-15 years

Cost Components Breakdown

Typical Mid-Range Installation ($3,500):

  • LED fixtures and controller: $1,200-$1,600 (40-45%)
  • Installation labor (8 hours): $800-$1,000 (25-30%)
  • Electrical work and wiring: $600-$800 (18-23%)
  • Permits and miscellaneous: $100-$200 (3-5%)
Note: Costs vary based on home size, roof complexity, and distance from electrical service.

Installation Cost Factors

Home Size Impact:

  • Small home (1,500 sq. ft.): $2,500-$3,500
  • Average home (2,000-2,500 sq. ft.): $3,500-$4,500
  • Large home (3,000+ sq. ft.): $5,000-$7,000

Roof Complexity:

  • Simple single-slope: Standard pricing
  • Multiple slopes/peaks: 10-15% additional cost
  • Complex architectural features: 20-30% additional cost

Electrical Distance:

  • Close to panel: Standard cost
  • 50+ feet: $300-$500 additional (trenching)
  • 100+ feet: $800-$1,200 additional

Premium Systems Pricing Details

TrimLight (Perimeter Ribbon System):

  • Material cost: $1,500-$2,500
  • Labor (perimeter installation): $1,200-$1,800
  • Controller and setup: $400-$600
  • Total typical: $4,000-$5,000

Gemstone (Modular Accent System):

  • Material cost: $1,200-$1,800
  • Labor (8-10 lights typical): $800-$1,200
  • Smart controller and setup: $600-$1,000
  • Total typical: $3,500-$5,000

OELO (Smart LED System):

  • Material cost: $1,600-$2,500
  • Labor (10-15 nodes typical): $1,000-$1,400
  • Smart controller and setup: $800-$1,200
  • Total typical: $4,000-$5,500

Comparing to Holiday Lighting

Traditional Holiday Lights (5-year cost):

  • Light sets replacement: $1,000-$1,500
  • Professional installation: $1,000-$2,000
  • Electricity (6 weeks/year): $500-$750
  • 5-year total: $2,500-$4,250

Permanent Lighting (5-year cost):

  • Initial installation: $4,000
  • Electricity (year-round): $1,000-$1,500
  • Maintenance: $500-$750
  • 5-year total: $5,500-$6,250
  • Plus: Year-round security and convenience

Hidden Costs to Consider

  • Building permits: $50-$200 (varies by municipality)
  • Electrical panel upgrade (if needed): $1,000-$2,000
  • Smart home hub (if not owned): $200-$400
  • Annual maintenance inspections: $100-$200
